About Travel hikers


Established in 2019

_________________________________________________________________

Travel hikers is one of Pakistan’s largest tour sellers and specialises in organising tours tailored to the specific interest, needs and requirements of every in – and outbound client. The Travel hikers head office is located in Karachi, with branches in Rawalpindi as well as Lahore, and together we have more than 60 employees, who ensure that a wide range of services are made available to all of our customers.
